Our Process

We’re Committed to Maintain Standard Work Process

We hand-pick fresh produce and carefully sort out any damaged or unsuitable items to ensure only the highest-quality harvest moves forward. After picking, everything is gently dried to maintain crispness and prevent excess moisture. The produce is then triple-washed in clean, potable water for safety and consistent quality. Once prepared, each batch is packed in clean, food-safe bags and clearly labelled with harvest and batch information. Throughout the entire process, from storage to delivery, the products are kept within a strict 1–4°C cold chain to maintain maximum freshness until they reach you.
